Super Sales Analysis Dashboard

An interactive Power BI Business Intelligence Dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, regional trends, and product performance. This project transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights through dynamic visualizations and KPI tracking, enabling faster and data-driven decision-making.

📌 Project Overview

The Super Sales Analysis Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of business performance using Microsoft Power BI. The dashboard allows users to monitor sales, profit, customer count, average delivery time, payment methods, product categories, customer segments, and regional performance through interactive visualizations.

🎯 Business Problem

Businesses often face challenges in monitoring sales performance across multiple regions and customer segments. Traditional reports make it difficult to identify profitable products, customer preferences, and seasonal trends.

This dashboard addresses these challenges by providing a centralized, interactive reporting solution that supports faster and more informed business decisions.

🎯 Project Objectives
Analyze overall sales performance.
Track key business KPIs.
Compare sales across regions and states.
Identify top-performing categories and products.
Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
Support business decision-making using interactive dashboards.
